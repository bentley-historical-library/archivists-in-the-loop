## High-Level Schedule

The symposium will take place over two days. For those traveling in from out of town, we recommend
arriving on Wednesday night. The bulk of the activities will take place on June 4 and 5.
All symposium discussions and activites, apart form a dinner on Thursday, will
take place at the Dow Event Space at the School of Information (see more details below).

### Thursday, June 4

We will begin at 9:00 a.m. with an ice breaker and opening remarks to frame the conversations.
Lunch and coffee will be provided for symposium participants.

Structured workshop activities will take place all day until 4:30, with a short wrap-up at 5:00 p.m.

An informal discussion, networking, and reflection dinnner will follow in the evening.
Please note that the dinner will likely be held at a local restaurant (not on campus).

### Friday, June 5

We will begin at 9:00 a.m. with discussions shaped in response to the first day of the symposium.
Lunch and coffee will be provided for symposium participants.

We plan to conclude the formal symposium at around 2:30, to allow
those who desire to return home the chance to do so.
For those who would like to stay in Ann Arbor on Friday,
we will provide some activity suggestions and
organize a "no-host" social activity for informal networking.

## Symposium Location

The symposium will be held at the Dow Event Space at the School of Information (on the fourth floor of the Leinweber Computer and Information Science Building at 2200 Hayward Street).
Please find [information about UMSI's location here](https://www.si.umich.edu/about-umsi/getting-umsi).
